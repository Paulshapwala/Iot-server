IoT-Based Temperature and Humidity Monitoring System

The high cost of proprietary embedded systems and software can create significant barriers to access and hinder progress in third world countries.

Our project aims to bring the benefits of IoT technology to communities in developing countries by making use of open source technology.

Project Overview

The system is designed to provide accurate and reliable environmental data that can be used to monitor the condition of crops in a greenhouse.
Greenhouses are a suitable impelementation of the IoT environmental monitoring system as they represent a closed system with limited external interfaces.

Data Collection

Our system collects data on a daily basis and stores it in our database; this can be accessed by clicking on 'daily data'.
